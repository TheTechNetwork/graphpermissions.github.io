RoleManagementAlert.ReadWrite.Directory

Allows the app to read and manage the role-based access control (RBAC) alerts for your company's directory, on behalf of the signed-in user. This includes managing alert settings, initiating alert scans, dimissing alerts, remediating alert incidents, and reading alert statuses, alert definitions, alert configurations and incidents that lead to an alert.
